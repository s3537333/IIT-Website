Overview and Motivation

The project idea will be to develop a smartphone app that would allows user to browse and purchase products such as clothings and accessories. This app would enable users to shop anywhere and at anytime with the product or products purchased being delivered to their doorstep. The app's target users would be between the ages of 12 -24 years old. According to Statistica, 95% of respondents between age 12-24 years reportedly owns a smartphone thus this is a huge market that can be tapped into. The shopping app can be used to regualrly update existing customers through push notifications which could contain exclusive offers and discounts. Furthermore, it is more convenient to have a shopping app as it enables you to purchase a desired product fast no matter where you are. This can be seen in a survey conducted by Go-Globe which is a web design company based in Hong Kong where 72% of consumers think that mobile apps make shopping easier and are less time consuming. In addition to that, Go-Globe found that there are an average of 4.2 shopping apps per smartphone owner which shows that there is great potential for a shopping app to be successful in the market.
