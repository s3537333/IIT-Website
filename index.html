Overview and Motivation

The project idea will be to develop a smartphone app that would allows users to buy and sell high end luxury goods such as designer watches,clothes, bags, footwear, jewellery and other accessories. The industry's revenue has grown 9.1% annually and has a revenue of $2.2 billion spread across 240 businesses. This increase is due to a growing demand from inbound tourist which has boosted the demand in the industry. Furthermore, Australia has a high number of international students with a majority of it being Chinese students from mainland China which is also a contributing factor in the booming of the luxury goods industry in Australia. According to an article published by the South China Morning Post, Chinese students and tourists are responsible for two thirds of luxury retail sales in Mebourne and Sydney. Therefore, it can be a market to tap into in which retailers and customers are able to connect with each other virtually thus allowing customers the freedom of purchasing luxury products without having to leave their home. It can not only connect retailers with Australian customers but also international customers in order to broaden their customer base. Furthermore, in recent years, the development and advancement of technology has seen an increase in smartphone ownership especially those between the age of 12-24 years old as evidenced through Statistica where 95% of respondents had smartphones. Statistica also reported that the number of smartphone users has reached 2.32 billion as of 2017 with an expected increase to 2.87 billion by 2020. In addition to that, a survey conducted by Go-Globe which is a web design company based in Hong Kong where 72% of consumers think that mobile apps make shopping easier and are less time consuming and an average of 4.2 shopping apps is present in a smartphone.

Description

The app will have several features including a push notification feature in order to regularly update our existing users about the latest products as well as exclusive offers and discounts.This feature would be beneficial as it increases sales and customer engagement. Furthermore, the app will introduce a loyalty scheme in order to attract new and retain existing customers.The app would offer points for every purchase which can in turn be exchangeable with e-vouchers for free delivery and discounts such as those offered by ASOS. Personalisation will aslo be offered through app settings to users as it can help in increasing custoemr experience as well as app value. Additionally, the app would offer 24/7 customer support by establishing strong communication with customers. Customer support will be offered through a chatbot or live chat with a staff member to help customers with any enquiries. Lastly, the app would have an easy payment feature in which customers can store their payment information in the app for future purchases in order to speed up transactions. Various payment method will be ofered such as credit card or debit card, Apple Pay, Paypal and many more.

Tools and Technology

Software

Xcode

Xcode is an integrated development environment made for iOs systems that contains software development tools developed by Apple for developing software for iOs.

iOS SDK

A software development kit developed by Apple Inc that allows for the development of mobile apps on Apple's iOS operating system.

Java

Java will be used to develop the app for Android devices as it is the official language for Android systems. It is easy to handle and many open source libraries are made available for users to choose from when developing the app.

Android SDK

Enables app developers to create applications for Android platform. The Android SDK typically includes sample projects along with source codes, an emulator, development tools and required libraries to build Android applications.

Apache Ant

An open-source tool that automates aspects of the Android app building process.

Gradle

An advanced build toolkit that manages dependencies and allows app developers to define custom build logics.

PhoneGap

An Adobe tool that is made avaiable for multiple platforms including iOs, Android and Windows with a framework included that wraps HTML5 apps in native containers for applications that require web applications for mobile platforms. This tool also helps the app access numeroud hardware features that smartphones offer and connect them to our app.
